NotiSend

Reliable Notification Delivery via Email

About the App

NotiSend is a simple and secure tool for sending notifications via email from your devices or systems to your inbox. Whether you're monitoring servers, tracking tasks, or receiving alerts, NotiSend delivers reliable email updates in real time.

Key Features

  • Send notifications using your custom SMTP or Google account (via OAuth2)
  • Supports multiple email providers with encryption
  • Clean and easy-to-use configuration interface
  • Works in the background without draining battery

Use of Data

NotiSend does not collect, store, or share any user data. All email credentials are stored locally on your device and are only used to authenticate and send messages through your configured email service provider.

If you choose to use your Google account for sending emails, authentication is handled via OAuth2 in accordance with Google's policies. The app never accesses or stores your password.
